How I Work

We slow things down enough to understand them.

Counselling does not need to be rushed, forced, or overcomplicated. We start with what feels important and work from there.

What sessions are like

Sessions offer a private space to pause, speak honestly, and begin to make sense of what has been going on.

I will not push you to talk about anything before you are ready. We can explore things steadily and carefully, without pressure to have all the answers straight away.

The clarity loop

A simple thread runs through my work: when life feels confusing, it helps to slow down the space between what happened and what we decide to do next.

Observe: what actually happened?

Separate: what is evidence, and what is interpretation?

Regulate: what is my capacity today?

Decide: what is the smallest honest next step?

Reflect: what did I learn from what happened next?

This is not about positive thinking. It is about accurate thinking, self-trust and a more secure way of relating to yourself, other people and uncertainty.

What we might explore

We might look at anxiety, self-doubt, relationships, boundaries, difficult decisions, emotional patterns, neurodivergent experience, or the things that feel hard to explain elsewhere.

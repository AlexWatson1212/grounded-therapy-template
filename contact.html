Contact

Get in touch.

If you would like to arrange an initial conversation or ask a question, you are welcome to send a simple message.

Email

hello@alexanderwatsoncounselling.co.uk

There is no pressure to explain everything in your first message. You can simply say that you are interested in counselling and would like to arrange a time to speak.

Before you contact me

Counselling is not an emergency service, and I may not be able to respond immediately. If you are in immediate danger, feel unable to keep yourself safe, or need urgent support, please contact emergency services, NHS 111, Samaritans on 116 123, or your local crisis service.

What can be customised?

Not fixed. Not generic. Built around you.

This demo gives you a starting point. From there, the site can be adjusted so it feels like your practice, not someone else’s template.

Logo & name

Replace the demo identity with your practice name, logo and visual style.

Colours & typography

Adjust the palette and type style so the site feels warmer, softer, bolder or more minimal.

Photos

Use your own room, portrait, nature imagery, abstract visuals or carefully chosen placeholders.

Sections

Add, remove or reshape areas such as fees, supervision, online therapy, FAQs or availability.

Copywriting

Rewrite the words around your voice, approach, clients and therapeutic values.

Contact flow

Link to email, enquiry forms, booking tools or a simple first-session process.

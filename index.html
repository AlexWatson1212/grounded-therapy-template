What I can help with.

People come to therapy for all kinds of reasons — sometimes a clear one, sometimes just a sense that things aren't quite working.

Anxiety: overthinking, worry, or feeling unable to switch off.

Burnout: running on empty and finding it hard to keep going.

Grief and change: loss, transition, or life feeling different than it did before.

Relationships: patterns that keep repeating at home, work, or elsewhere.

Feeling stuck: not quite knowing what is wrong, only that something feels hard.

Whatever brings you here, the aim is the same: to feel a little less alone with it, and a little clearer about what comes next.

What working together is like.

There's no script and no pressure to have it all figured out before we start.

A little about me.

I'm a qualified counsellor with experience supporting adults through anxiety, loss, relationship difficulties and periods of change.

My approach is warm and unhurried. I won't hand you a diagnosis or a formula — I'll listen properly, and we'll work out together what actually helps.

I believe people often carry more of their own answers than they realise. Sometimes they just need a steady person and enough space to hear them.
